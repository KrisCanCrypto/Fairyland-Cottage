My Journey to Simple, Sustainable Living
Audio & ebook

In this book I share my journey to simple, slow and sustainable living in the Irish countryside. The book is filled with stories from bustling city life to the Austrian hills, from shaky beginnings to the present day. Spanning a period of twenty years it shows the steps leading to Fairyland Cottage life, a life consisting of less waste and more calm. I outline what I’ve learnt along the way and share zero waste tips for home and garden. In the second part of the book I include my favourite recipes for cleaning, cooking and personal care.
